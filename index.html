Hip-hop duo NEONBLACK, formed by Saynt Gavi and Thom Khemet, is based out of Santa Ana, California. Fueled by a love for music and a passion for creating, they have been working together from a very young age. After much experimentation, NEONBLACK has crafted a unique sound that draws from a diverse source of influences consisting of everything from hip-hop and electronic to indie and pop music. Their music often touches on themes of existential angst and a struggle with identity and attempts to meld an artistic point of view with the hip-hop narrative.
